Music and drama classes

Music classes provide a wide range of benefits for individuals of all ages and skill levels. Whether you're learning to play an instrument, sing, or study music theory, participating in music classes can have a positive impact on various aspects of your life.

At the same time, drama classes offer a well-rounded educational experience that enriches the student's personal and social development while nurturing their creativity and self-expression.

Singing classes

Singing classes focus on training and developing the singing voice, offering a wide range of benefits to individuals of all ages and skill levels.

Whether you're pursuing singing as a hobby or a potential career, singing classes can provide a structured and supportive environment for improving your singing abilities and enjoying the many personal, social, and artistic benefits that singing has to offer.

Piano classes for beginners

Piano classes for beginners are designed to be friendly with a cozy and relaxed atmosphere.

Teaching the piano in a supportive manner can foster a positive and productive learning environment, making the experience enjoyable and effective for students.

Companies - Team Day

Music and drama classes for company team days offer a unique and engaging experience to foster teamwork and creativity.

Participants will collaborate through interactive improvisational exercises, script readings, and ensemble performances.

Improvised Echo will become your safe place where feelings will be better understood, integrated, and accepted.

Here is where music and drama will bring tools to help you along your journey.
