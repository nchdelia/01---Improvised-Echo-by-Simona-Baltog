Companies - Team Day

For companies - Team Day

Description

In my music and drama classes for company team days, I offer a unique and engaging experience to foster teamwork and creativity. Participants will collaborate through interactive improvisational exercises, script readings, and ensemble performances.

These activities are designed to enhance communication, build trust, and ignite the creative spirit within your team. Join me for a memorable team-building day where the power of music and drama brings your company closer together, creating lasting bonds and a positive, dynamic work environment.

Keypoints

  1. Group Improv Sessions
  2. Team-building Scenes
  3. Corporate Storytelling
  4. Interactive Icebreakers
  5. Corporate Songwriting
  6. Office-based Skits
  7. Role Reversal Exercises
  8. Leadership Role-Playing
  9. Musical Team-building Games
  10. Improvised Presentations
  11. Creative Problem-Solving
  12. Expressive Movement
  13. Team Song Performances
  14. Feedback and Reflection Circles
  15. Closing Ensemble Performance
